You can now change your speech to text that can be updated in your blog instantly without having to insert it using your keyboard.
== Description ==
1. You can convert your speech into text without actually typing the content. 
2. You can adds content to your website just through speech.
3. Chrome is necessary for the functioning of this plugin.
4. Microphone is the hardware required.
5. speech to text
6. voice to text
7. voice to word
8. Hands Free Typing

== Installation ==

1. Copy the entire /simple-speech-to-text/ directory into your /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
2. Activate the plugin.
3. Posts or Pages have one new section called "Simple Speech to Text".
4. Attached Microphone and then perform.
